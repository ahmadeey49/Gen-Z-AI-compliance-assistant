- FRSC (Road Safety): Citizens must provide driver's license and vehicle papers. Seatbelts are mandatory.
- CAC: Business registration is required for SMEs. It protects your brand name.
- FIRS: Companies must pay VAT and CIT. Tax Identification Number (TIN) is free.
- Human Rights: Citizens have the right to remain silent until they see a lawyer.
- Police: No citizen should be tortured. Bail is free.
